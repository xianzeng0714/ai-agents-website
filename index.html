Welcome to AI Agents World

AI agents are intelligent entities that perceive their environment and take actions to maximize their chances of success. They can be used in various applications, from simple task automation to complex decision-making processes.

This website provides information about AI agents, their types, and how you can get in touch with us.

Types of AI Agents

Simple Reflex Agents

Simple reflex agents react directly to percepts. They have no memory of past states and their actions are based solely on the current percept.

Model-Based Reflex Agents

Model-based reflex agents maintain an internal state that depends on the percept history and thereby reflect at least some of the unobserved aspects of the current state.

Goal-Based Agents

Goal-based agents use goal information, which describes situations that are desirable, to make decisions. They combine goal information with a model of the world.

Utility-Based Agents

Utility-based agents try to maximize their own "happiness". A utility function maps a state to a real number, which describes the associated degree of happiness.
